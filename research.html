The Physical-Digital Loop

Digital twins are tightly coupled with their physical counterparts in a continuous feedback loop: the physical system informs the digital twin via sensors, while the digital twin informs physical control.

We develop hierarchical frameworks that couple continuous high-fidelity models with data-aware machine learning to maximize accuracy subject to computational constraints.
